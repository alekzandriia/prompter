Prompt

Prompt is a tool that creates prompts by randomly combining two ideas. Because they are generated randomly, these combinations can at first appear ridiculous or non-sensical. This is actually where the magic happens! Prompt's ideas are not limited by what we, as humans, might think of as compatible or even possible in the real world. Prompt can help you generate ideas that transcend. Get out of your creative rut and get inspired with Prompt.
